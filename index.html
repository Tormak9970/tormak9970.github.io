About

TORCommunity.com is a fan site for Star Wars The Old Republic. It was started in 2008, and has many neat features, such as guides, walkthroughs, tips, tools, and multiple databases. The sites goal is to provide great resources, and improve the experience players have while playing the game.

My Involvement

I got involved with TORCommunity after the owner and head dev of the site reached out to me while I was working on the Character and Outfit Designer and asked if I would be willing to put it up on TORCommunity. He had been looking for a tool just like it for the site for a while, and I was hoping to get it up on one of the fan sites as well. After finishing the app, and putting it up live on the site, he offered me the opportunity to continue working on the site, which I gladly took him up on. Since then, I have continued to work on the Character and Outfit Designer, as well as other parts of the site.

Organizations: SWTOR Extractors

About

The SWTOR Extractors, Modders, and Dataminers (or SWTOR Extractors for short) Collaboration is a group who enjoys taking Star Wars The Old Republic beyond the game. Whether this be through using models for fan films, or creating tools for the community (such as my Character and Outfit Designer), the community enjoys finding new and unique was to utilize the game.

My Involvement

I was became a collaborator (essentially I work on new tools and techniques for the community, and help people whenever they have questions) because of my work on the Character and Outfit Designer. This was because I added a secondary feature to my tool, the ability to download a .zip file with all of the nessesary information to recreate the character in Blender. This, combined with a script I wrote to automatically locates all the models and textures, takes a process that used to take over 1 hour, and makes it take under 20 minutes. Since finishing that, I continue to help people, as well as work to make the process even easier.

SWTOR Character and Outfit Designer

Overview

This is one of my favorite projects. It is an external character creator and outfit designer for Star Wars The Old Republic. I really love this game and the community, and wanted to do something everyone could use and enjoy. It is very complexed, and I had to learn alot of new topics and skills while working on it. Im also very proud of it since it is the first tool of its kind made by anyone, ever, and that required me to solve alot of problems no one else has done before.

Reasons & Purpose

  • I wanted to make something truly unique that had not been done before
    • This is the only Star Wars The Old Republic external character designer
  • I also wanted to make something people would not only want to use, but also something that would inspire people, and help them realise what they can do.
  • I had read comments and posts on a variety of platforms from people requesting/asking about a character viewer/creator, the earliest dating back to around 2014
  • I also wanted to expedite the process from re-creating your character outside of the game (for 3D modeling, printing, or just for fun).
    • The process could take upwards of one to two hour for one character
    • With my program, it takes ~20 minutes

What I learned

  • How to read hexadecimal headers
  • How to read custom file formats
  • Basics of PHP
  • Advanced JavaScript
  • How to better manage the layout of my code
  • How to better structure and organize my time
  • How to break down problems into achievable sections
  • How to effectively troubleshoot issues and debug
  • Sometimes the best solution is to take a break and work on different part/something else for a while
  • How to use the THREE.js library
  • Basics of 3D design/modeling
    • HLSL
    • GLSL
    • The graphics pipeline (how 3D models are shaded and rendered)
    • How models work
    • How textures work
    • How lighting works
    • How shadows work
